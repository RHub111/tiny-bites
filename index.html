Why Early Nutrition Matters

The first 1,000 days of life — from conception through age two — are a critical window for brain development, immune health, and lifelong relationship with food. What babies eat (and how they're offered food) creates lasting habits.

Healthy Eating Tips

Simple, evidence-based principles for raising adventurous little eaters.

🌈

Eat the Rainbow

Offer fruits and vegetables in as many colors as possible. Different colors mean different nutrients, antioxidants, and phytonutrients essential for growth.

🔁

Repeated Exposure

It can take 10–15 exposures before a toddler accepts a new food. Keep offering without pressure — neutral, relaxed presentation is key.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Meals Together

Babies learn by watching. Eating the same foods together removes the "other food" stigma and builds positive food associations.

🚫🍬

Delay Added Sugar & Salt

Avoid added sugars and sodium for babies under 12 months. Their kidneys can't process excess salt, and sweet preferences form quickly.

🤲

Baby-Led Weaning

Letting babies self-feed soft finger foods from around 6 months builds motor skills, independence, and reduces picky eating later.

💧

Hydration Matters

Introduce small sips of water with solid meals at 6 months. Avoid juice — whole fruit provides the same nutrients with added fiber.

Feeding by Age

Every stage brings new milestones and new foods to explore.

0–6
months

Milk Stage

Breast milk or formula provides 100% of nutritional needs. No water, juice, or solids needed yet. Focus on responsive feeding cues.

🤱 Breast milk   🍼 Formula
6–9
months

First Foods

Purées or soft mashed foods. Introduce iron-rich foods first (meat, lentils, fortified cereals). Offer one new food every 3–5 days.

🥕 Purées   🫘 Lentils   🍗 Meat
9–12
months

Finger Foods

Soft finger foods cut to pea-size. Introduce common allergens (peanut, egg, dairy). Encourage self-feeding to build pincer grasp.

🥑 Avocado   🍳 Egg   🧀 Cheese
12–24
months

Family Foods

Transition to whole cow's milk. Join family meals with adapted textures. Expect picky phases — stay calm and keep variety on offer.

🥦 Broccoli   🍝 Pasta   🫐 Berries
2–3
years

Toddler Explorer

Independence peaks — toddlers assert preferences strongly. Offer structured meals/snacks, maintain routines, and keep meals low-pressure.

🌽 Corn   🍠 Sweet potato   🥩 Strips
